Privacy Policy

MyGarden Match-3 is a prototype game. The web version stores player name, score, coins, stars, lives, settings, and progress locally in your browser.

If a backend URL is configured, the game may send player name, score, level, and progress data to that backend for leaderboard and cloud-save features.

No real-money purchases, ads, or third-party analytics are active in this prototype. If those are added later, this policy and the Play Store Data Safety form must be updated.
